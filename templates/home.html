Be part of the future


We have a new AR business card tool! Our platform, simple, fast and easy to use, will allow companies to create and design their own business cards for their employees in real time and be able to view them at the moment.


How ir works?


1. Register your company

Your company will have its own user to manage all the cards


2. Complete the data of your employees

You can update the data of your employees whenever you want


3. Customize your card designs

You will have the freedom to choose the design of the cards individually for each employee


4. Ready! A trigger will be automatically generated for each


Technology

Lightweight

Our platform uses AR.Js, a lightweight yet powerful library capable of creating augmented reality for Mobile and Web with just a few lines of code.

Web App

There is no need to download any apps for use, making it instantly scalable and upgradable.

Customizable

You will be able to design and personalize each card yourself and it will be created on the spot.

Fast

Our A-Frame + AR.Js augmented reality engine works with the DOM, but its elements do not touch the browser's layout engine.

Multi Platform

The web app is compatible with all types of devices and any operating system. It can be viewed from a smartphone to a PC.

Green Responsability

Thanks to the fact that the information on the cards is updatable, this means that the card does not have to be reprinted for each change that you want to display.
